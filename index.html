Board Members


Eric Meng

My name is Eric. I am a second year at the University of Chicago, majoring in economics and psychology. I co-founded The First Draft for two main reasons. I love creative writing. After taking a creative writing course my first year, I realized this is a great activity that allows me to express my ideas through other characters and see what I write come together as a story that others can enjoy as well. The second reason is that I want to extend this passion of mine to give an opportunity and safe space for kids from disadvantaged backgrounds to do the same. I have talked to these high school students who want to learn to write creatively and express their feelings and thoughts without judgments but lack the opportunity to do so. They  ultimately motivated me to carry this project out. A fun fact about me is that I enjoy traveling to other countries to experience different cultures. I have been to around 30 countries so far and hope to continue to add to this total.
